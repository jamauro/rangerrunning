Run / Walk

Guided Walk / Run Tours

Guided tours of Austin allow participants to exercise and explore in the capitol city. The tours break down into small groups that are personable, informational and flexible. Different routes and themes of tours that are offered include: Brewery, Historic, Music, Haunted/Bats, University. The tours move at a casual pace that allows for photo stops and conversation.

  • Location: Begins at hotel/conference venue and tours cover different parts of Downtown Austin
  • Duration: 45-60 min
  • Equipment: None during the day/ Running lights at night

5k Fun Run

A casual and inclusive 5k fun run in which Swift Fit manages course creation, signage, staffing, permitting, lead and trail runners, insurance/waivers and an emcee. This is meant to match all fitness levels and is an event that works seamlessly with any clients who wanted to add a 5k run/walk to their agenda.

  • Location: Begins at hotel/conference venue or Trinity and Cesar Chavez and takes place on Lady Bird Lake Trail
  • Duration: 45-90 min
  • Equipment: Course creation, signage, staffing, permitting, lead and trail runners, insurance/waivers and an emcee
  • Enhance: Photos, start/finish line, truss, hydration, EMT, awards/medals, course maps, shirts, tracking chips

Downtown Timed Run

This timed run allows runner’s times to be tracked via RFID chip or sports clock. The timed run has a few more bells & whistles as far as amenities and also has the option of adding: Photos, start/finish line, truss, hydration, EMT, awards/medals, course maps, shirts, tracking chips. This option is a level up from the fun run in terms of production.

  • Location: Begins at hotel/conference venue and takes place on Lady Bird Lake Trail
  • Duration: 45-75 min
  • Equipment: Course creation, signage, staffing, permitting, lead and trail runners, insurance/waivers and an emcee
  • Enhance: Photos, start/finish line, truss, hydration, EMT, awards/medals, course maps, shirts, tracking chips

Mindful Movement & Meditation

Mindful Movement

These classes are aimed at supporting participants in cultivating a deeper mind-body awareness. The aim here in not intense physical exercise, per-se, but more so movement to encourage balance, inner peace, clarity, and increased mental well-being. Appropriate for all levels from beginner to advanced.

  • Location: Hotel/conference venue or a green space/terrace if weather permits
  • Duration: 60 min
  • Equipment: Mats

Yoga: This style yoga will be more gentle than the group fit component. Here participants will engage in Yin or Restorative Yoga. Yin Yoga is a slow-paced style of yoga as exercise with asanas (postures) that are held for longer periods of time.

Tai Chi: Tai chi is an ancient Chinese tradition that, today, is practiced as a graceful form of exercise. It involves a series of movements performed in a slow, focused manner and accompanied by deep breathing. Often described as meditation in motion, tai chi promotes serenity through gentle, flowing movements.

Kundalini Yoga: Kundalini Yoga uses breath, movement and focus to energize the body while supporting strength and balance of the immune and glandular systems. Dynamic classes promote emotional well-being, mental clarity and stress relief. Appropriate for all abilities. This will be the most dynamic of all three mindful movement offerings.

Meditation

Swift Fit’s Meditation offerings are appropriate for all levels. Our meditation classes are perfect for stress management and encourage the busy, on the go professional to unplug for a moment and get their energy and mind centered so they can take on the day. Meditation classes can be 30 or 60 minutes.

  • Location: Hotel/conference venue or a green space/terrace if weather permits
  • Duration: 15/30/60 min
  • Equipment: None

Meditation Class: This class is designed to bring participants through a particular meditation. Meditations will be designed based off of the client’s goals. Unless otherwise specified by the client, meditation classes will be guided meditations where the students are guided by the teachers voice, set to soothing background music.

Full Meditation Class: Meditation + Self-Reflection: Just like the meditation class, but with an added bonus at the end! Once the meditation is complete, students will be given the space to dive into self-reflection through guided journaling. Journaling combined with meditation is proven to open up creativity and promote deep clarity.  *Due to the nature of this class, it can only be offered as a 60 minute option.

Relaxing Sound Bath: This is a sound meditation. Students will be invited to sit in a meditative posture or to lay down while listening to the soothing sounds of a variety of sound healing tools: like crystal singing bowls, gongs, and chimes. Students will typically be guided in a light breath- work exercise to prepare the body and mind at the beginning of class and then will be guided into deep relaxation.  Benefits of a sound bath are: Reduced stress and anxiety, decreased blood pressure, quiets mental chatter, increased focus, and promotes increased health.

Breathwork / Pranayama: The breath is a fundamental tool when it comes to mindfulness. Pranayama is defined as an ancient technique focused on the regulation of the breath through certain exercises. The average rate of breathing for most people is about sixteen times per minute. When the rate of breathing increases, or if it becomes rapid and irregular, the mind also becomes disturbed and erratic. This class will give students practical breathing techniques to apply in daily life. Students will learn and experience how to manipulate the breath into different patterns to affect their mental state in a variety of positive ways.
